# Simple Bank Account Management Program

Hey there! This is a simple Python program that helps you manage bank accounts without any fancy database stuff. It's super straightforward, so let me guide you through how to use it.

## How to Use

1. **Create an Account:**
   - Choose option 1 from the menu.
   - Enter the account holder's name and initial balance.
   - Ta-da! You've just created a bank account.

2. **Deposit Money:**
   - Select option 2.
   - Enter the account number you want to deposit money into.
   - Specify the amount you want to deposit.
   - Boom! Your account now has more money.

3. **Withdraw Money:**
   - To take some cash out, hit option 3.
   - Enter your account number again.
   - Type in the amount you want to withdraw.
   - If you've got enough in the account, you'll have cash in hand.

4. **Check Balance:**
   - Curious about your account balance? No problem.
   - Select option 4.
   - Provide your account number.
   - You'll get your balance instantly.

5. **Exit the Program:**
   - Done with banking? Choose option 5.
   - You'll gracefully exit the program.

## Important Notes

- Account numbers are automatically assigned, so you don't need to worry about them.
- If you enter an invalid account number, the program will politely let you know.
- If you withdraw more than you have, it'll also tell you that your pockets aren't deep enough.
- This program doesn't use any databases, so all data is stored in memory. If you close the program, your accounts will disappear!

Feel free to use, modify, and expand upon this program as needed. Enjoy your simple bank account management adventure!
